Our Firewood

Premium hardwoods for every application — residential and commercial.

White Oak

Dense, long-burning, and low-smoke. A top choice for fireplaces and wood stoves.

Oak

Classic hardwood with excellent heat output. Versatile for home heating and outdoor fire pits.

Hickory

The gold standard for BBQ and cooking fires — rich aroma, intense heat, and bold flavor.

Seasoned Hardwoods

Properly dried mixed hardwoods ready to burn. Great value for high-volume needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is your firewood seasoned?

Yes. Delivery is available throughout our service area — Atlanta, North Georgia, Western North Carolina, Upstate South Carolina, and much of the Southeastern US.

Yes. Every order is delivered and neatly stacked at your location — no extra work for you.

What wood species are available?

We carry White Oak, Oak, Hickory, and Seasoned Hardwoods. Kiln-dried wood is also available for commercial cooking applications.

Yes. We proudly serve restaurants, pizza ovens, BBQ restaurants, and other commercial kitchens. Commercial wood is typically cut to 18-inch lengths and split to order.
